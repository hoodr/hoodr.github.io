About Me

My name is Drew, I'm a senior Computer Science B.S. with a Math minor at Boston College, and very excited to join the engineering team at Blue Apron after graduation. For the last two years, I've been very interested in the field of machine learning and A.I. after discovering it through a course I took in Big Data. So far I only have two public projects (Gutenberg and Simpsons), but I'm working on a few more. I am also working on a rather large multi department Virtual Reality project which you can find more information on my homepage. Outside of computer science I'm an avid photographer and one time photo editor of The Heights, The Independent Student Newspaper of Boston College (and the only newspaper on campus). Check out my photos section for some of my work. Among other things I also love to hike, run, surf, camp, travel etc.
